About Me

Hi! I'm Abigail, a final year biology student at the University of the West Indies, Trinidad and Tobago, with a passion for anime and fish. I created this website to share a little bit about myself and my interests. I hope you enjoy exploring it as much as I enjoyed making it!

In my free time, I love watching anime, especially series that blend fantasy and science fiction. I'm also an avid fish enthusiast, fascinated by the diversity of aquatic life. Whether it's a colorful betta or a majestic angelfish, I find joy in learning about and caring for these beautiful creatures. I like going on long walks for no reason. Overall, I'm just a curious and creative person who loves to explore the world around me.

- Abigail
